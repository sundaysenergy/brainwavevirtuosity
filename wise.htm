Anna Wise EEG and Protocol

The Awakened Mind/High Performance Mind protocol uses an EEG called the Mind Mirror. It was originally designed by Maxwell Cade and Geoffrey Blundell and is now built by Thought Technology. (See pictures this page.) Here the brainwave display is on a vertical axis with the fastest waves at the top (Beta) and amplitude measured from the center out. The right and left side of the screen correspond to the left and right hemispheres of the brain. In this protocol the relationship of the different clusters of brainwaves are observed and trained. Psychologist Anna Wise designed the Awakened Mind/High Performance Mind protocol. She has developed a unique bio-monitoring process which is a real time interaction with brainwave patterns and the content of the mind. Ms. Wise also is a bineural beat composer – creating music to assist the client to move into the brainwave frequencies of meditation. This music is often playing in the background during sessions. Ms. Wise also has a series of CDs providing guided meditations that clients can do at home to reinforce the brainwave sessions. Dr. Vazquez also specializes in developing personalized guided visualizations to enhance session work.
