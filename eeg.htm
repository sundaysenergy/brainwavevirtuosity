Neurofeedback Protocols

OPEN FOCUS™ and Neural Synchrony Protocol

In Dr. Fehmi’s Neural Synchrony protocol the NS-500sx electroencephalograph is used. Dr. Fehmi designed this EEG to train phase synchronized alpha brain waves between the frontal, temporal, occipital and parietal lobes. When this whole brain synchrony occurs the client sound and light feedback. Dr. Fehmi’s research on how to heighten the functioning of the brain also included research in accessing this optimal state without the EEG. What Dr. Fehmi discovered was that the best way to move into whole brain synchrony was to use the imagination in an effortless way to sense space, volume and distance in and through different sense perceptions.

Because the OPEN FOCUS™ CD’s work synergistically with the neurofeedback, clients also listen to one Dr. Fehmi’s sequential CDs during the session. There are a broad range of exercises covered in three levels of training:

Level 1 “Skills for Physical and Mental Mastery”
Level 2 “Alleviating Stress-Related Physical and Emotional Pain”
Level 3 “Optimizing Performance and Function”

In addition, Dr. Vazquez is able to create OPEN FOCUS™ exercises tailored to the client’s specific needs in the session. With repeated brainwave training sessions and practice with the OPEN FOCUS™ exercises, the client achieves greater flexibility and stability of brainwave state. Awakened Mind/High Performance Mind Protocol

The Awakened Mind/High Performance Mind protocol uses an EEG called the Mind Mirror. It was originally designed by Maxwell Cade and Geoffrey Blundell and is now built by Thought Technology. Here the brainwave display is on a vertical axis with the fastest waves at the top (Beta) and amplitude measured from the center out. The right and left side of the screen correspond to the left and right hemispheres of the brain. In this protocol the relationship of the different clusters of brainwaves are observed and trained. Psychologist Anna Wise designed the Awakened Mind/High Performance Mind protocol. She has developed a unique bio-monitoring process which is a real time interaction with brainwave patterns and the content of the mind. Ms. Wise also is a bineural beat composer – creating music to assist the client to move into the brainwave frequencies of meditation. This music is often playing in the background during sessions. Ms. Wise also has a series of CDs providing guided meditations that clients can do at home to reinforce the brainwave sessions. Dr. Vazquez also specializes in developing personalized guided visualizations to enhance session work.
