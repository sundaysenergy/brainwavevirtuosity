Personality Traits that Perpetuate Anxiety
 (From The Anxiety and Phobia Workbook, E. Bourne)

  • Perfectionism
    • Holding unrealistically high expectations of yourself & others
    • Over involvement/obsession with mistakes
  • Excessive need for approval
    • Stems from inner sense of being flawed
    • Always looking outside of self for validation
  • Tendency to ignore physical and psychological signs of stress
    • Out of touch with body, inability to recognize signs of stress
    • Pushing beyond mental, physical, emotional limits
  • Excessive need for control
    • Needing to have everything be predictable
    • Vigilance, inhibits ability to trust and let go (which flow requires)
Research has proven that there is a direct correlation between reduced stress, wellness and overall performance. Dr. Anna Vazquez coaches clients to access the state of mind and corresponding brainwave pattern that is most beneficial for the activity they want to enhance, whether it is learning, creating, performing, or accessing the “deep peace inner circuitry” of healing and restoration.
